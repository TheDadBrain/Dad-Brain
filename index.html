Privacy Policy
How Dad Brain handles account data and private notes.
Effective date: April 24, 2026

This draft is provided for launch readiness and should be reviewed by a qualified attorney before public release.

Information We Collect

Dad Brain collects the information needed to create and operate your account, including your email address, password authentication data handled by our authentication provider, optional profile details you choose to add such as your birthday, notes, reminders, folder names, links, support requests, and basic usage events used to improve the app.

How We Use Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Dad Brain.
  • Save and sync your notes, reminders, account settings, and folders.
  • Respond to support requests and account questions.
  • Measure feature usage, diagnose issues, and protect the service.
  • Process subscriptions or founder access when enabled.

Private Notes

Your notes are meant to be private. We do not sell your notes. We do not use your notes for advertising. We only access account content when needed to provide the service, troubleshoot, comply with law, prevent abuse, or respond to your request.

Sharing

We may share limited information with service providers that help us run the app, such as hosting, database, authentication, analytics, support, payment, and email providers. These providers are expected to use information only to provide services to Dad Brain. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

Payments

When paid plans are enabled, payment information will be handled by a third-party payment processor. Dad Brain should not store full card numbers.

Data Retention

We keep account information and notes while your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You can delete notes inside the app. You can request account deletion or data help by contacting support.

Your Choices

  • Update profile details in Profile or Settings.
  • Delete notes and reminders in the app.
  • Export or copy notes where available.
  • Request help with account deletion or access by contacting support.

Children

Dad Brain is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13. Do not create an account for a child under 13 or submit personal information directly from a child under 13.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No online service can guarantee perfect security.

Changes

We may update this policy as Dad Brain changes. The effective date will show when the policy was last updated.

Contact

Questions or requests can be sent to thedadbrain@gmail.com.
